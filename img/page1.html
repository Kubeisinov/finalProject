Qazaq Republic Scholarship for Needy Groups

The Qazaq Republic Scholarship is awarded to citizens of the Republic of Kazakhstan recognized by the admissions committee or CDU applicants in the relevant specialty or included in the list provided by the admissions committee, and students who meet one or more of the following categories:

- orphans or children left without parental care; - disabled people of I, II and III groups; - persons from large families; - persons with one or both disabled parents of I, II and III groups.

The scholarship is funded by the Qazaq Republic. There is no provision for payment or compensation for reduced fares. The scholarship is awarded for the entire period of study in the relevant program.

The public association “Kostanay graduates” is an association of alumni “Kostanay educational and innovative lyceum”, created in order to unite the graduates of the lyceum and create an effective network to support graduates, exchange experience, by funding initiatives and projects. Students of SDU who graduated from “Kostanay educational and innovative lyceum”.

- Maximum number of fellows per semester is 5 students

- The amount of the monthly scholarship is 20,000 tenge
